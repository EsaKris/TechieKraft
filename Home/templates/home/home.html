Inspiring Tomorrow’s Innovators, Today

TechieTeen bridges the gap between education and technology. From guiding students into the world of digital innovation to helping educators and school owners adopt cutting-edge tools, TechieTeen is your partner in shaping the future of education. Elevate your school, book a free session with us.

About TechieTeen

Who We Are

TechieTeen is a flagship program by the Learning Arm of Techiekraft, designed to empower high school students, teachers, and school administrators with knowledge about the boundless opportunities in digital technology.

The program introduces students to various tech fields, helping them see how they can position themselves for future careers in digital technology. For teachers and school owners, TechieTeen demonstrates the immense benefits of adopting digital tools to streamline school management, making day-to-day operations more efficient.

Why TechieTeen?

TechieTeen stands out as a one-of-a-kind program that combines education, empowerment, and opportunity:

Live Engagement

Unlike conventional programs, TechieTeen provides a hands-on, live interaction that ensures students actively engage with real world insights. For teachers and school owners, it shows them a first hand practical on how these digital tools work and how to effectively use them.

Exposure to Opportunities

By introducing students to technology early, they are better positioned to become the innovators and leaders of tomorrow.

Capacity building for Teachers

Teachers have the opportunity to upskills theirsleves from the session and turn out to be more productive.

Global Recognition

Supported by partners like Winumu, Lampnets Solutions, and School360 LMS, TechieTeen offers students and schools exposure to globally accredited opportunities, partnerships, and tools.

What to Expect from TechieTeen

For Students

  • Inspiring Sessions: Hands-on, interactive discussions to help students see the vast opportunities in the tech ecosystem.
  • Career Guidance: Practical advice on how to position themselves in various digital fields.
  • Scholarships and Incentives: Access to scholarships and resources from TechieTeen partners.

For Teachers and School Owners

  • Digital Tools Awareness: Insights into how tools can simplify school management tasks like finance, CBT, and results management.
  • Efficiency Demonstration: Real-life examples of how adopting tech tools can save time and improve performance.
  • Access to Solutions: A first-hand look at Techiekraft’s products, designed to transform school operations.
  • Enhanced Online Presence: Hosting schools benefit from tools and resources to boost their visibility and credibility online.
  • Access to free and trial tools for school management, such as financial management software, CBT applications, and result management systems as a result of partnering with Techiekraft.
  • Improved online presence.
